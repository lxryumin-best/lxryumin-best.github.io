Verified Expertise — A Guarantee of Reliability for Your Business#

When choosing an IT partner, it is essential to be confident in their qualifications. My certificates are not just documents — they are official proof that I possess current technologies at a professional level.

A certified specialist means: verified knowledge, officially confirmed by the equipment or software manufacturer. You are not simply hiring an “experienced IT guy,” but a specialist who has passed rigorous assessment.

MikroTik Certified Security Engineer (MTCSE) Certificate

The MikroTik Certified Security Engineer (MTCSE) is an advanced-level MikroTik certification in the field of network security. It confirms expertise in building multi-layered protection for corporate networks: configuring firewalls, intrusion detection and prevention (IDS/IPS), DDoS mitigation, building secure tunnels, and organizing safe access to corporate resources. For your business, this means: professional protection of your corporate infrastructure from external threats, compliance with information security requirements, and minimized risk of data breaches.

MikroTik Certified Network Associate (MTCNA) Certificate

The official MikroTik certification confirms deep expertise in designing, configuring, and administering RouterOS routers and switches. Covers building secure enterprise networks, VPN tunnels, firewalls, and traffic management on MikroTik equipment — one of the world standards in telecommunications hardware.

Kaspersky Lab Authorized Specialist Certificate

The authorized Kaspersky Lab certificate confirms professional competencies in deploying, configuring, and administering Kaspersky Endpoint Security corporate cybersecurity solutions. Ensures business protection against modern cyber threats: viruses, ransomware, data breaches, and network attacks.
