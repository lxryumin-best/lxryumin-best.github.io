My expertise spans the entire IT stack—from the physical layer to advanced virtualization:

  • Network Architecture & Physical Infrastructure: Designing, planning, and deploying local area networks. My most extensive hands-on project involved engineering a network utilizing over 4,500 meters of UTP cable.
